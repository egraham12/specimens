This lightweight assignment serves two purposes: to demonstrate comfort with the fundamentals of DOM modification and to start building the final project code. It lays the foundation, scaffolds the outline, and otherwise prototypes the final project code. Nearly all of the assignment requirements are within the click function. In the code, they're referenced with comments that say "Assignment 3," so that you can use ctrl+f to find them a little easier.

Starting with the inner ring, this visualization represents the taxanomic breakdown (phylum, class, order, and family) of some sample records from the Museum of Comparative Zoology. The sizes represent the relative number of specimens compared to the total holdings. Although the colors make it hard to tell (that's a work in progress), each arc does line up with its higher designation (i.e. if you follow the line breaks between phylums outward, they encapsulate their child designations).
